Negative Numbers



❮ ❯


Numbers can be positive or negative.


Here's a number line that should look very familiar. It starts at 0, then counts up by 1 starting from there:




We know that if we were to keep going to the right, we would have 1, then2, and so on. What happens if we keep going to the left of 0 ? We get negative numbers!
To the left of 0 is -1, -2, -3. It's as simple as that!

Adding and Subtracting

Two like signs become a positive sign. Two unlike signs become a negative sign


Example:

Like signs:

3+(+2) = 3 + 2 = 5
5+(+1) = 5 + 1 = 6

Unlike signs:

7+(−2) = 7 − 2 = 5
8−(+2) = 8 − 2 = 6

Addding negative numbers is the same as subtracting.
Subtracting negative numbers is the same as adding.
